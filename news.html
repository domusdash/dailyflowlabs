LocalRedactPDF: Bulletproofing Document Privacy locally in WebAssembly

Most online PDF editing and redacting tools require you to upload your sensitive contracts, tax forms, or medical logs to their remote cloud servers. This leaves a persistent trail of personal identification information (PII) vulnerable to data breaches.

To address this risk, we have released LocalRedactPDF. By combining browser-native HTML5 Canvas manipulation with high-performance WebAssembly compilation, the app lets you redact documents with zero server exposure.

Why it's awesome: It runs 100% offline. The built-in regex scanner automatically highlights SSNs, email addresses, and phone numbers. Once you confirm, the pixels are flattened directly onto the document canvas, making the underlying text completely uncopyable and mathematically unrecoverable.

Security Release • June 24, 2026 • 3 min read

ThumbVerify: Intelligent Cryptographic Verification powered by Gemini AI

In an era of rising supply-chain attacks and corrupted payloads, ensuring the integrity of downloaded files and digital documents is crucial. Yet, command-line checksum tools are too complex for average users, and standard web utilities lack the ability to analyze file contents intelligently.

Enter ThumbVerify, an intelligent verification workspace. It pre-hashes your media files locally for speed, then securely streams them to the Gemini API to analyze visual artifacts, check metadata, and output a detailed, human-readable integrity report.

Why it's awesome: It bridges cryptographic safety with AI intelligence. While checksums ensure files aren't corrupted, Gemini inspects EXIF details and pixel distributions, detecting AI generation, editing, or potential tampering. You get a complete safety summary automatically, without needing to decipher raw command-line hex values.

Developer Tool • June 23, 2026 • 3 min read

ShortCodeIcons: Turbocharging Web Vitals (LCP) with Optimized Inline SVG Assets

Page load speeds directly impact both search engine rankings (SEO) and user conversion rates. Often, the culprit behind high Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) delays is loading external icon fonts (like FontAwesome) or bloated, unoptimized third-party CDNs.

To help developers speed up their web designs, we built ShortCodeIcons. It is a curated, sub-kilobyte directory of inline SVG paths, equipped with automated path minification.

Why it's awesome: It includes an interactive customization panel to tweak strokes, sizes, and colors in real-time. With a single click, it outputs optimized SVGs formatted for HTML, React/JSX, Tailwind, or Markdown, eliminating external font payloads entirely.
